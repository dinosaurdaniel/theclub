DanBot

DanBot is a wonderful bot with plenty of capabilities including the generation of random advice and soup recipes, a gauge of a user's morality, and so much more!

To date, DanBot has been invited to 7 servers, and this figure increases weekly.

You could even invite DanBot here if you wanted to...

TravelBot

TravelBot was a Discord utility designed to aid members of the Travelers community.

With TravelBot, you can view portions of The Travelers's natural terrain, check a dynamically updating leaderboard, and even play minesweeper! :o

If you're interested in downloading TravelBot, just visit this link.

Kaythan Engine

It's much easier to precisely communicate your creativity through plaintext than with convoluted graphics. That's why I love terminal games!

The Kaythan Engine is a work in progress which hopes to expose the wonders of programming with prepared development kits for UI, processing and databases.

It's a beginner's dream come true!

Planned Projects

LinkDan

LinkedIn is an inarguably amazing success, with more users than platforms as well-known as Tumblr. Students, however, are often neglected on this platform.

That's why I'm aiming to create a clone of LinkedIn directed towards my personal peers, as a close-knit media sharing group with publicly available portfolios and activity feeds. I call it LinkDan, for reasons nobody may ever understand.

Islands

Using the Kaythan Engine with inspiration from games like The Travelers and UnReal World, I've been developing an open-source terminal-based MMORPG with elements from dozens of games.

I hope to create a wonderful experience for users, and, considering current progress, I'm confident I will!
